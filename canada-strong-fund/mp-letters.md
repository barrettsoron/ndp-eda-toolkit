# Letters to Your MP: Canada Strong Fund

*Especially Liberal MPs. Constituent pressure during the legislative window.*

**Vancouver Centre primary template; adaptable for any riding**

---

## Why we're writing MPs

The Canada Strong Fund's enabling legislation is being drafted now. The Transition Office is being stood up. The first board appointments are weeks away. **The window for shaping the design is open until the cement sets** — and the people inside their own caucuses have the most leverage. Liberal backbenchers in particular hear from whips when their ridings start writing letters; the volume itself is a political signal.

This is constituency pressure, not persuasion. Don't expect to flip your MP's vote. Do expect to put on-record asks that contribute to the political environment they're operating in.
