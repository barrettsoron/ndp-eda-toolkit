# Canada Strong Fund — NDP EDA Organizing Kit

Assets for federal NDP riding associations to push the caucus, Leader's Office, and policy apparatus to adopt a sharp position on PM Carney's Canada Strong Fund (CSF) — the $25-billion arm's-length Crown corporation announced 2026-04-27.

This module differs from the Regina and Surveillance Pricing modules: it is a **national-scope policy critique**, not a riding-specific opposition campaign. The "Pull a Norway" framing — Avi Lewis's response to the Spring Economic Statement — is the spine.

> *"Norway uses industry profits to support the public good. Prime Minister Carney's fund uses public money to support private profits."*
>
> — Avi Lewis, on the Spring Economic Statement, 2026-04-28

## Tactics in scope

Five tactics for the legislative-window organizing push:

1. **Letters to the Editor** → `letters-to-editor.md`
2. **Social posts** → `social-copy.md`
3. **Summer street canvassing** → `canvass-guide.md` + `petition.md` + `fact-sheet.md`
4. **Small-group meetings/conversations** → `small-group-guide.md` + `fact-sheet.md`
5. **Write MPs — especially LPC MPs** → `mp-letters.md`

## Scope

This is a **national policy fight** that local EDAs can move forward through five direct-action tactics during the legislative window (Spring Economic Update through enabling legislation): letters to the editor, social posts, summer street canvassing, small-group conversations, and MP letters — especially to LPC MPs.

## Branding

All materials follow NDP federal branding conventions per [ndp.ca/branding](https://ndp.ca/branding). This is a **party** action by riding associations — not a leadership campaign initiative.
