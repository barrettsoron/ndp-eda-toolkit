# Data Centre Petition — Vancouver Centre NDP

Assets for the community campaign to pause TELUS's proposed three-site, 150-megawatt AI data centre cluster in British Columbia — two proposed new data centres in Vancouver, plus an expansion of the existing TELUS facility in Kamloops — announced 2026-05-13 as the first project under the federal *Enabling Large-Scale Sovereign AI Data Centres* program. The downtown Vancouver site is in pre-application stage with no formal rezoning submitted to the City as of May 18, 2026.

This is a riding-level initiative on the unceded territories of the **xʷməθkʷəy̓əm (Musqueam), Skwxwú7mesh (Squamish), and səlilwətaɬ (Tsleil-Waututh) Nations**. All materials use NDP federal branding.

The downtown site at 150 West Georgia / 720 Beatty St — the largest of the three, beside BC Place — is in **Vancouver Centre** (federal riding, MP Hedy Fry, LPC). The Mount Pleasant site at 111 East 5th Ave is in **Vancouver East** (MP Jenny Kwan, NDP). This module is maintained by Vancouver Centre NDP; coordination with Vancouver East NDP is a first-tier next step.

## E-Petition

These materials support **physical signature collection only**. To set up an electronic petition:

- **Through ndp.ca:** Work with the BC regional organizer to request an e-petition page on the federal NDP platform. This gives you centralized data collection, NDP branding, and integration with the national CRM.
- **Locally:** Build your own using a privacy-respecting form tool. Ensure you comply with the federal NDP Privacy Policy and Canadian anti-spam legislation (CASL) for any email follow-up. Signatures collected electronically should follow the same data custody protocols as physical forms.

Either way, the petition copy in `petition.md` can be adapted for digital use.

---

## Data Custody

The Vancouver Centre NDP electoral district association has custody of all data collected through this petition, governed by the federal NDP Privacy Policy.

- Personal information is used to contact signatories about NDP activities and shared with riding associations as needed.
- Personal data is **never sold**.
- Signatories can opt out at any time: dnc@ndp.ca | ndp.ca/privacy
- Canvassers must **not** photograph, screenshot, or store personal data on personal devices.
- Signed paper petitions should be returned to chapter leads or brought to EDA events.
