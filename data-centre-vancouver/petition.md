# Pause TELUS's Vancouver AI Data Centres

**Vancouver Centre NDP | Unceded Musqueam, Squamish, and Tsleil-Waututh territory | Physical signature collection**

---

## Summary

TELUS and Westbank — the Vancouver development firm founded and led by billionaire Ian Gillespie — want to build two AI data centres in Vancouver: 100 megawatts beside BC Place at 150 West Georgia downtown, and a repurposed facility at 111 East 5th Avenue in Mount Pleasant. The AI companies that will actually rent the compute have not been publicly named. Of the announced $9 billion in economic activity, a substantial share is GPU procurement from NVIDIA in California — chips, not local capital. The province is currently importing dirty electricity. Metro Vancouver is in continuing drought. We are calling on the federal government to pause this build until our communities have a say.

---

## Petition statement

*"I call on the federal government to pause TELUS and Westbank's two Vancouver AI data centres — at 150 West Georgia downtown and 111 East 5th Avenue in Mount Pleasant, announced May 13, 2026 — until the AI companies set to rent the compute have been publicly named, and until free, prior and informed consultation with the Musqueam, Squamish, and Tsleil-Waututh Nations has taken place. Most of the money in this deal will leave Canada; the grid load, the water cost, and the downtown land will not. Our energy, our water, and our democratic process must serve people who live here — not Ian Gillespie's Westbank, and not the unnamed AI companies that will run on this infrastructure."*
