## Context

Lorneville sits within the traditional territory of the **Wolastoqiyik** (Maliseet) and **Peskotomuhkati** (Passamaquoddy) peoples, with overlap into **Mi'kmaq** territory. Unlike Treaty 4 (numbered treaty, surrender-and-reserve framing), the **Peace and Friendship Treaties** of the 1700s did not surrender land or resources. This shapes how a campaign should approach consultation questions — Indigenous title and jurisdiction in the Maritimes was never extinguished by treaty.
