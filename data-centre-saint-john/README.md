## E-Petition

These materials support **physical signature collection only**. To set up an electronic petition:

- **Through ndp.ca:** Work with your regional organizer to request an e-petition page on the federal NDP platform. This gives you centralized data collection, NDP branding, and integration with the national CRM.
- **Locally:** Build your own using a privacy-respecting form tool. Ensure you comply with the federal NDP Privacy Policy and Canadian anti-spam legislation (CASL) for any email follow-up. Signatures collected electronically should follow the same data custody protocols as physical forms.

Either way, the petition copy in `petition.md` can be adapted for digital use.

---

## Data Custody

The Saint John–St. Croix NDP electoral district association has custody of all data collected through this petition, governed by the federal NDP Privacy Policy.

- Personal information is used to contact signatories about NDP activities and shared with riding associations as needed.
- Personal data is **never sold**.
- Signatories can opt out at any time: dnc@ndp.ca | ndp.ca/privacy
- Canvassers must **not** photograph, screenshot, or store personal data on personal devices.
- Signed paper petitions should be returned to chapter leads or brought to EDA events.

## French Translation

New Brunswick is Canada's only constitutionally bilingual province. Acadian French speakers are concentrated in the north and east of the province; Saint John itself is majority anglophone but the riding draws on a provincial-scale French-speaking constituency. A French translation of these materials — particularly the petition, fact sheet, and social copy — is a **first-tier** future step, not optional.
