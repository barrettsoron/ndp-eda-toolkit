# Pause Beacon's Lorneville Data Centre

*Their Gas. Our Wetlands. Our Choice.*

**Saint John–St. Croix NDP | Peace and Friendship Treaty territory | Physical signature collection**

---

## Summary

Beacon AI Centers (Calgary) and VoltaGrid (Texas) are proposing a 390 MW data centre campus at Lorneville, in Saint John — drawing 190 MW from an on-site natural gas plant and 200 MW from NB Power's grid — with no disclosed anchor tenants, no meaningful community consultation, and the destruction of high-functioning wetlands and old-growth forest on Peace and Friendship Treaty territory. We are calling on the federal government to pause the project until our community has a say.

---

## Petition statement

*"I call on the federal government to pause the expansion of AI data centres in Canada — starting with Beacon AI Centers' proposed 390 MW campus at Lorneville, Saint John — until meaningful community consultation, environmental assessment, and democratic oversight have taken place. Our energy, our wetlands, our water, and our data sovereignty must serve Canadians first."*
